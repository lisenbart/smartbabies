About SmartBabies

Edutainment for preschoolers — stories, songs, mini‑quests and learning shorts.

Who we serve

Target audience: ages 2–5. We design for early childhood attention spans with clear repetition, rhythm and positive behavioral cues.

Focus: Edutainment + Emotional IQ. Core skills (letters, numbers, colors, safety) reinforced through simple social‑emotional learning.

What we produce

Format: 3–5 minute episodic content optimized for YouTube and short‑form platforms.

Mission: Empower preschoolers to explore with kindness and curiosity through joyful, safe stories led by our space captain hero, ScoopyCap.
